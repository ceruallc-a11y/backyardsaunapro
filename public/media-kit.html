About the Site

Backyard Sauna Pro publishes detailed, research-backed buying guides for people actively shopping for home saunas, sauna heaters, and sauna accessories. Every page targets a specific buyer intent keyword — from "best barrel sauna kits" to "Harvia KIP 60B review" to "outdoor sauna for sale."

The site covers the full purchase journey: brand comparisons, product reviews, installation guides (electrical, foundation, ventilation), and accessory recommendations. Content is written for buyers who are 2-4 weeks from making a purchase decision.

Why We're a Good Fit

Backyard Sauna Pro reaches buyers at the exact moment they are comparing brands and products — not casual browsers. A reader on our "Best Barrel Sauna Kits" or "Redwood Outdoors Review" page has already decided they want a sauna. They are choosing which one. That is the highest-intent traffic in the sauna category, and it is the traffic most likely to convert on a partner's product page.
